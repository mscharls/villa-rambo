Villa Rambo

C A N G U U

Villa Rambo is situated in the quiet village of Tumbak Bayuh. Away from the hustle and bustle of downtown Canggu, this two bedroom villa boasts a roof-top Balinese pavillion with rice paddy views. Watch the sunrise and sunset from this unique location all whilst relaxing in a architecturally designed western style home. Two sizeable bedrooms, each with extra-wide queen size beds and private ensuite will ensure you rest well. The living area includes a third guest bathroom, kitchen and open plan lounge area that looks out over the patio and private pool. The property is fully enclosed and includes secure parking for you and your guests.
